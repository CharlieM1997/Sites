About Us




Style Stomps Footwear was established in the UK in 2015, launching several main stores in key areas over England, Wales and Scotland, and has been gradually expanding since then.

Slowly and surely Style Stomps has been expanding and opening countless new stores across the country; our goal is to provide customers with the best quality shoes and the newest styles, which can more than rival more established stores. In a short space of time, Style Stomps has became on-par with them.

Now with the release of our new website, Style Stomps continues to push forward into the next generation of fashion and style! As well as selling our own unique range on-site and in-store, we are also proud to sell premium brands such as Nike, Clarks, Addidas, Converse and many more.
