Returns Policies



    Returns - Excluding Web Exclusive Items

  • If for any reason you are not happy with your purchase, items can be returned for a refund or exchange within 14 days providing they are returned in their original condition and with proof of purchase.

  • Home delivery items can be returned to your local store (this does not include any independent concessions) or for free to our Head Office using Collect Plus.

  • Store delivery items can be returned to your local store (this does not include any independent concessions) but cannot be returned to Head Office using Collect Plus.

  • BFPO orders can be returned from within the UK using Collect Plus or to your local store (this does not include independent concessions).

  • Refunds made in-store will be credited to your original payment card.

  • Refunds made at Head Office will be credited to your original payment card within 3 to 5 days. Only the purchase price of the items returned will be refunded, not the delivery charge. This does not affect your cancellation rights.



    Returns - Web Exclusive Items

  • If for any reason you are not happy with your web exclusive purchase, you can return for a refund within 14 days providing they are returned in their original condition and with proof of purchase.

  • Web exclusive items cannot be exchanged.

  • Web exclusive items cannot be returned to any store.

  • Web exclusive items can only be returned for free to our Head Office using Collect Plus.
